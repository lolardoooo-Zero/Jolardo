Company Story

From a spare room to a senior team of 40

Jolardo was founded on a simple frustration: marketing agencies were selling creativity and hiding the numbers behind it. Our founders — a media buyer and a brand strategist — set out to build a company where every EGP of spend could be traced to a result.

Today we work with retail groups, hospitality brands, and fintech startups across Egypt, running strategy, media, and production out of one accountable team.

We stayed intentionally mid-sized. Big enough to run national campaigns, small enough that founders still review every account personally.
